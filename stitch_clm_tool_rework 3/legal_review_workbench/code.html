Master Services Agreement

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into as of the Effective Date by and between Acme Corp, a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Innovation Drive, Tech City, CA ("Client"), and LegalSuite Inc., a California corporation ("Provider").

1. Services and Scope

1.1. Scope of Work. Provider agrees to perform the professional services described in one or more Statements of Work ("SOW") attached hereto or subsequently executed by the parties (collectively, the "Services"). Each SOW shall be subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

comment

1.2. Change Orders. Client may request changes to the Services by submitting a written request. Provider shall respond within five (5) business days with an assessment of the impact on fees and timeline. No changes shall be effective unless agreed to in writing by both parties.

2. Compensation and Payment

2.1. Fees. Client shall pay Provider the fees set forth in the applicable SOW. Unless otherwise specified, invoices are due net thirty (30) days from the invoice date. All amounts are payable in Indian Rupees (INR) unless otherwise stated.

2.2. Late Payments. Any late payments shall bear interest at the rate of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less.

3. Intellectual Property

3.1. Client IP. Client retains all right, title, and interest in and to its Confidential Information and any materials provided to Provider for the purpose of performing the Services.

3.2. Provider IP. Provider retains all right, title, and interest in and to its pre-existing intellectual property, tools, methodologies, and any improvements thereto developed during the course of the Services.
